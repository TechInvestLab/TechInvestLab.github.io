Organization for Organizers

TechInvestLab.ru is a partnership of policy and IT advisers, specialized in reforms, market adaptation and restructuring for large corporations.

Our partners are working together from early 90's and have many years of experience in strategy and IT consulting, including first Russian IPO of internet provider in 1992, first web-studio in the middle of 90's, participation in E-government initiatives and adaptation of engineering companies to the 4th Industrial Revolution..

We are deeply involved with systems engineering, life-cycle management and life-cycle data integration in energy, construction, shipbuilding. Our System Thinking and System Engineering education courses are highly valued by both students and experienced engineers.
